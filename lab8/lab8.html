Garlic Prawns

Ingredients

  • 500g large prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for added heat)

Instructions

For Garlic Prawns:

Ensure the prawns are peeled and deveined. Pat them dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture. Season them lightly with salt and black pepper.

Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

Once the prawns are cooked through, drizzle lemon juice over them in the skillet. Give everything a gentle toss to coat the prawns evenly with the garlic and lemon mixture. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed. Garnish the garlic prawns with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of freshness and color.

Transfer the garlic prawns to a serving dish or individual plates. Serve immediately while still warm, either as an appetizer or as part of a main course. Enjoy your delicious garlic prawns!

Laisa Shirataki

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of shirataki noodles (about 200g)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced

Instructions

Rinse the shirataki noodles under cold water and drain well. If they have a distinct odor, you can boil them for 2-3 minutes to remove it. Set aside.

In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Set aside.

Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add sliced onion and cook for another minute until softened. Add diced tofu and cook until lightly browned. Add sliced bell pepper, julienned carrot, and sliced mushrooms. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender but still crisp.

Transfer the Laisa Shirataki to serving plates. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds. Serve hot and enjoy!

Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.

2. Add sliced chicken breast and stir-fry until cooked through.

3. Add sliced bell pepper and onion to the skillet and cook until vegetables are tender-crisp.

4. Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce, and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Cook for another minute.

5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Crunchy Bubble

Ingredients

  • 2 bread, sliced
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons coco powder
  • sugar and milk

Instructions

1. Arrange alternating slices of bread and mozzarella cheese on a serving plate.

2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the bread and mozzarella slices.

3. Drizzle coco glaze over the salad.

4. Season with sugar and cinamon to taste. Serve immediately.
